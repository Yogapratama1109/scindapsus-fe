What does the name mean ?

Scindapsus Mangurai is taken from the name of a princess who comes from the story of the origin of the city of Jambi where this type of Scindapsus Argyraeus Variegated (Mangurai) originated.

Growth Habit

A slow grower It likes bright indirect light Humidity is best around 60-80% Like more watering Should have a good air circulation

Leaf Characteristics

This Scindapsus Mangurai is a Scindapsus Argyraeus which has the characteristics of thick and velvety leaves from the usual leaves.

Varigation Characteristics

Scindapsus Mangurai is a mutation of Scindapsus argyraeus where the original character of the argyraeus is lost due to mutations in the varigata, the varigata character has tricolor (green, white and blue) and because of this makes mangurai unique.
